## Getting Started

### How do I use Relaxess?

1. **Download the app** from Apple App Store or Google Play
2. **Select your mood** from the home screen (Anxiety, Stress, Overthinking, Sleep, Sadness, or Just Relax)
3. **Start a session** to chat with our AI assistant
4. **Explore tools** like breathing exercises, sleep mode, and ambient audio
5. **Customize** your language and theme in Settings

### What devices are supported?

- **iOS:** iPhone 12 and later (iOS 14+)
- **Android:** Android 8.0 and later
- **Web:** Available through most modern browsers

## Technical Support

### Microphone Not Working

**Q: Why can't I use voice input?**

1. Check that you've granted microphone permission:
   - **iOS:** Settings → Relaxess → Microphone
   - **Android:** Settings → Apps → Relaxess → Permissions → Microphone

2. Restart the app
3. Check your device's microphone is working (test with another app)

**Q: How do I revoke microphone permission?**

- **iOS:** Settings → Relaxess → Microphone → Turn Off
- **Android:** Settings → Apps → Relaxess → Permissions → Microphone → Deny

### Audio Not Playing

**Q: Why isn't audio streaming?**

1. Check your internet connection
2. Ensure you have enough device storage
3. Restart the app
4. Try a different audio track
5. Restart your device

**Q: Why is audio buffering?**

- Your internet connection may be slow
- Try connecting to a stronger Wi-Fi network
- Close other apps using bandwidth
- Download audio for offline listening (Premium only)

### App Crashes or Freezes

**Q: What should I do if the app crashes?**

1. Force close the app
2. Restart your device
3. Update the app to the latest version
4. Clear the app's cache (Settings → Apps → Relaxess → Storage → Clear Cache)
5. Reinstall the app if problems persist

**Q: How do I clear app cache?**

- **iOS:** Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Relaxess → Offload App (or Delete App)
- **Android:** Settings → Apps → Relaxess → Storage → Clear Cache

### Performance Issues

**Q: The app is slow. What can I do?**

1. Close other running apps
2. Restart your device
3. Update to the latest app version
4. Check available device storage (free up space if needed)
5. Disable background app refresh for non-essential apps

## Troubleshooting

### General Issues

**Q: The app won't open**

1. Force close the app
2. Restart your device
3. Update the app to the latest version
4. Reinstall the app

**Q: I'm not receiving notifications**

1. Check notification settings:
   - **iOS:** Settings → Relaxess → Notifications
   - **Android:** Settings → Apps → Relaxess → Notifications

2. Ensure notifications are enabled for the app
3. Check your device's Do Not Disturb settings

**Q: The app is using too much battery**

1. Close the app when not in use
2. Disable background app refresh
3. Reduce screen brightness
4. Disable location services (if enabled)

### Account Issues

**Q: I forgot my password**

Relaxess doesn't require a password. Your account is managed through your device's app store account (Apple ID or Google Account).

**Q: How do I change my language?**

Open Settings → Language → Select your preferred language

**Q: How do I change between light and dark mode?**

Open Settings → Theme → Select Light or Dark

## Health & Safety

### Important Disclaimer

**Relaxess is not a medical service.** The app is designed for relaxation and wellness purposes only and should not replace professional medical care.

**If you are experiencing:**
- Suicidal thoughts
- Severe anxiety or panic attacks
- Depression or persistent sadness
- Medical emergencies

**Please contact:**
- Emergency services (911 in the US)
- National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988
- Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
- Your healthcare provider

### When to Seek Professional Help

Relaxess can support your wellness journey, but it's not a substitute for professional mental health care. Please consult a healthcare professional if you:

- Have a diagnosed mental health condition
- Are taking psychiatric medications
- Have thoughts of self-harm
- Experience persistent emotional distress
- Have medical concerns
