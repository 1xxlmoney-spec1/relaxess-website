## 2. When to Seek Professional Help

### 2.1 Immediate Emergency

**If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, STOP using Relaxess and seek immediate help:**

**In the United States:**
- **Emergency Services:** Call 911
- **National Suicide Prevention Lifeline:** 988 (call or text)
- **Crisis Text Line:** Text HOME to 741741
- **International Association for Suicide Prevention:** https://www.iasp.info/resources/Crisis_Centres/

**In Other Countries:**
- Contact your local emergency services
- Visit your nearest emergency room
- Call your country's mental health crisis line

### 2.2 Professional Help Recommended

Please consult a licensed mental health professional if you:

- Have thoughts of self-harm or suicide
- Experience severe anxiety, panic attacks, or depression
- Have a diagnosed mental health condition
- Are taking psychiatric medications
- Have a history of mental health treatment
- Experience persistent emotional distress
- Have experienced trauma
- Have substance use concerns
- Are in an abusive relationship
- Have any medical or psychological concerns

### 2.3 Conditions Requiring Professional Care

The following conditions require professional medical or mental health care:

- Bipolar disorder
- Schizophrenia or psychotic disorders
- Severe depression
- Severe anxiety disorders
- Obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD)
- Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD)
- Eating disorders
- Substance use disorders
- Personality disorders
- Suicidal or self-harm ideation

## 6. Specific Conditions & Warnings

### 6.1 Suicidal Thoughts

**If you are having thoughts of suicide:**

- DO NOT rely on Relaxess
- STOP using the app immediately
- Contact emergency services (911 in US)
- Call the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988
- Text HOME to Crisis Text Line: 741741
- Tell someone you trust immediately

### 6.2 Severe Mental Health Conditions

If you have been diagnosed with any of the following, please consult a mental health professional before using Relaxess:

- Bipolar disorder
- Schizophrenia
- Severe depression
- Severe anxiety
- Psychotic disorders
- Personality disorders
- Eating disorders
- Substance use disorders

### 6.3 Medication & Treatment

If you are:

- Taking psychiatric medications
- Undergoing mental health treatment
- In therapy or counseling
- Under psychiatric care

**Please consult your healthcare provider before using Relaxess.** Relaxess should complement, not replace, professional treatment.

### 6.4 Trauma & PTSD

If you have experienced trauma or have PTSD:

- Relaxess may trigger traumatic memories
- Relaxation exercises may not be appropriate for all trauma survivors
- Professional trauma-informed care is recommended
- Consult a trauma specialist before using Relaxess
